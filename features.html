Our Unique Offerings

Nature Immersion

Immerse yourself in the sounds and sights of nature with our guided forest walks, birdwatching tours, and stargazing experiences. Connect with the environment and rejuvenate your senses amidst the pristine surroundings.

Wellness Retreat

Unwind and rejuvenate at our wellness center offering yoga classes, meditation sessions, and spa treatments. Pamper yourself while embracing a holistic approach to well-being in a tranquil and natural setting.

Farm to Table Dining

Indulge in flavourful and robust delights prepared with locally sourced, organic ingredients. Our farm-to-table restaurant offers a culinary journey that celebrates the flavours of Uttarakhand, and supports the local economy.

Accommodation

Magpie offers an interesting choice of accommodations that includes cottages and tents, so your stay can be easily customised. Our cottages are very spacious that can easily accommodate your family, with ample space for your kids to hop around the floor. These cottage rooms offer King-size beds with ample natural light, and have attached bathrooms. Room amenities include a selected supplies of tea / coffee sets, water bottles, bath & skin products and personal items set in tastefully furnished spaces. (Extra bed available at nominal charges).

Our Tents are the most popular stay options. These tents are quite durable and offer a countryside wandering experience that is complemented by comfortable on-the floor mattresses, uninterrupted power & light, charging ports, easily accessible washrooms and breathtaking views of neighbouring mountains. Some of our customers first stay in cottages and then move to the tents to complete the experience. (Folding beds are available upon request at nominal charges).

All our guests are offered a welcome drink on arrival. Breakfast is a part of the package as an option. Assortment of packaged snacks and non-alcoholic beverages are available 24 hours on chargeable basis.

Dining

Our Food concept has evolved from the idea of sustainability in consumption. Most of our food, dairy products and ingredients are sourced locally. A variety of local organic fresh produce is used in our preparations. Our cooks bring together elements of local mountain cuisine of Uttarakhand, seasonal produce and their creativity to craft a rustic yet unique culinary experience.
