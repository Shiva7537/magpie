What is Magpie

Magpie was born from a simple desire: to reconnect, rediscover and rejuvenate. Our journey began from a dream for a quiet yet vibrant space, a place far from the chaos of the city; free of noise & pollution, distraction & demands of our daily lives. We wanted a nest from where we could watch the mist roll down the mountains at dawn, a cradle where we could feel comforted by gentle mountain breezes, a home where the night would bring a parade of bright stars to accompany our stories as we sit and chat with our guests. Simply put, a noiseless place where we could slow down, listen to our inner voice and rediscover life.

Magpie is that place - snuggled up in the lap of Himalayas, a laid-back corner in Mukteshwar, Uttarakhand. Magpie is not just a property; it's a commune, a philosophy, a whiff of freshness that enriches each moment that we spend here. It's a romance between Fragrance of woods and charming sunrays sneaking from tree-leaves. And it's a celebration of the land upon which it is built.

Our commitment to Sustainability

Magpie's commitment to sustainability stems from the thought of Indian values of-

  • "Vasudhaiv Kutumbkam" - the world is a family
  • “Mata Bhumi Putroham Prithivyah” - this earth is our mother, and we are her children.

Our primary approach is to construct only what is necessary, and let our guests experience the nature, explore the landscape and immerse in spiritual aspect of travel. We thoughtfully consider how we can enrich the spaces that we occupy, and while we elevate our spirits staying here, we also see how we improve this place. This philosophy is the key factor in designing of this property, where we have more open and natural spaces within and outside the property for you to explore.
