Our Story

Magpie Mountain Village in Mukteshwar, Uttarakhand, is our dream that we would love to share with everyone. We fell in love with this place the moment we saw it and we decided to let the world know about it. So here we are - giving this place a touch of what we do best - give care and warmth to our guests. Let your eyes soak the breathtaking views; let your ears enjoy the cooing and chirping of birds; let your mind relax as you cuddle up with your loved ones, or take a stroll around the walking trek.
